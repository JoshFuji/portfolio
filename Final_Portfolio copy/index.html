DEKOR

Dekoration Made Easy

Dekor is a decoration rental app designed to make event planning more sustainable and affordable. I designed Dekor to help users browse, reserve, and return decor items with ease—whether for birthdays, weddings, or seasonal celebrations. The app streamlines the rental process, encourages reuse, and empowers users to create beautiful events without the cost or waste of one-time purchases.
